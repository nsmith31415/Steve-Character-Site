My name is Steve.

I am the origional character of Minecraft. I wear a light blue shirt and blue pants, and I'm not afraid to get them dirty by mining, crafting, and creating.

I kill skeletons.

I hate all of the monsters that inhabit this world. It is my job to purge them and create a more peaceful land.

I mine diamonds.

My life revolves around mining. Diamonds are the highest reward found in the mines. I can create the best armor and weapons out of this pure gem.

I craft weapons

When I have the resources I need, I can craft weapons and armor to fit my needs, and to help me destroy the monsters that inhabit this world.
